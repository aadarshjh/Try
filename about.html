About Us

Who We Are

Welcome to VIT Connect! We are a team of passionate individuals committed to providing the best services to VIT students by centralizing vital information into a single platform.

Since our founding in 2024, we've been dedicated to creating solutions that help students navigate their day-to-day campus life more efficiently.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a comprehensive platform that centralizes services like maps, mess menus, shop listings, FAQs, and events into one convenient hub, streamlining information for the VIT student community.
